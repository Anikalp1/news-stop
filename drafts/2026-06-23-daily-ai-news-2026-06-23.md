# Quantum Health, Stack Wars, and Fugu Ultra

AI is expanding into high-stakes domains from oncology to geopolitics. New model releases and regulatory proposals are shifting how we build and pay for intelligence.

## Stack battles: the US-China artificial-intelligence rivalry is moving beyond chips alone - Bruegel

**What happened:**  
The competition between the US and China is shifting focus. The rivalry now extends beyond hardware and chip access to the entire AI stack.

**Why it matters:**  
Infrastructure dependencies are expanding. Developers and startups need to track where their full stack is sourced to avoid geopolitical supply chain risks.

**What happened:**
