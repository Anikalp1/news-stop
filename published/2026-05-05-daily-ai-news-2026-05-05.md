# Campus AI Degrees, Quantum Learning, and Agent Optimization Arrive

AI education and infrastructure are converging this week as universities launch specialized degrees while quantum computing enters the learning systems arena and AWS previews new agent optimization tools.

**What happened:**  
LPU announced details for its B.Tech Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning program launching in 2026, covering eligibility requirements, fees, and career prospects.

More AI-focused undergraduate programs mean a growing pipeline of developers entering the field with formal training in machine learning fundamentals.
