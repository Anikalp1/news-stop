## Mechanistic Analysis of Alignment Algorithms in Language Models

**What happened:**  
A new arXiv paper provides a mechanistic analysis of six preference‑optimization methods—PPO, DPO, SimPO, ORPO, GRPO, and KTO—across three open‑weight model families using layer‑wise linear probing.

**Why it matters:**  
Engineers gain visibility into how each alignment technique reshapes internal model computations, helping them pick the right method for specific fine‑tuning goals.

## From Senses to Decisions: The Information Flow of Auditory and Visual Perception in Multimodal LLMs

**What happened:**  
Researchers traced how audio and visual tokens move through multimodal LLMs to affect final predictions, exposing the internal pathways of sensory integration.

**Why it matters:**  
Understanding these routes lets developers design more efficient multimodal architectures and troubleshoot where modality contributions get lost or diluted.
